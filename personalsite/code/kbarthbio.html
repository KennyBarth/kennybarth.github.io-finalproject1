KENNETH BARTH

Biography

A little bit about me:

I was born in West Chester, OH in 1995.  Since I was a little kid, I have always been interested in computers and music.  When I was six I would try to design my own websites in Microsoft Word, just messing around with fonts and colors, which laid the groundwork for a lifelong passion towards computers and design.  My musical journey began when I was nine years old and started playing trombone, but I was always interested in rock music.  Then I picked up the guitar at age twelve and fell in love.  Since then I have played in four bands, and am currently one of the guitarists for the metal band Starless, along with my brother Joe.  Today I live in Covington, KY with my lovely girlfriend Vanna and our cats (check out the photo gallery to see them!)

Computer science

I started attending NKU as a Computer Science major in the spring of 2021.  Since then I have had a blast learning Python, HTML and CSS.  I'm looking forward to learning JavaScript so I can take my web development skills to the next level.  Aside from web development I want to learn to design software.  I have ideas for plug-ins and different things that would benefit musicians recording at home, and I'm excited to be able to implement these ideas.

Starless

Starless was born from the ashes of our old project Airways, which went through a lot of lineup changes, played a couple shows and wrote a few songs but never really got off the ground.  The main constants in that band were me, Joe and Dylan and in 2018 we got Wally on board as a vocalist and Ethan in on drums for the new project.  At that point, everything clicked that hadnâ€™t clicked with any of the original lineups, so we decided to rebrand, write new songs and Starless was born.  Today, we've released one EP and two singles and have played several great shows with bands like Hollow Front, Wolves at the Gate and more.  We're currently working on our second EP and have some cool shows planned for the fall!
